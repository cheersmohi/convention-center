The Vancouver Christmas Market Spreads Holiday Cheer and Raises $60,000 for Make-A-Wish Foundation

After a one-year hiatus, the Vancouver Christmas Market returned to the Vancouver Convention Centre’s Jack Poole Plaza in 2021 and had one of its most successful seasons yet. The German-inspired Christmas market started in 2010 and quickly became a beloved holiday tradition. Known for its incredible selection of food and festive shops, the Vancouver Christmas Market introduced new features this past year to ensure event-goers can celebrate the holiday season with family and friends, safely.
